Policies

Privacy & terms

What this platform is

MedMix is a community-reporting and educational tool. It is not a diagnostic, regulatory, or medical authority, and it does not replace professional medical advice, Poison Control, or emergency services.

What data we collect

Submissions to Counterfeit Watch and the Signal Board are anonymous by default. We do not require names, emails, or other identifying information to submit a report. Country-level location is used only to power the Global Risk Map.

How data is used

Aggregate, anonymized data may be shared publicly through the Impact Dashboard and Global Risk Map to support public health awareness. Individual reports are not sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Accuracy & limitations

Community-submitted reports are unverified at the time of posting. The Verified Reference Database is separately curated from cited public sources and clearly distinguished from user submissions throughout the platform.

Emergency situations

If you believe you or someone else has been exposed to a dangerous chemical combination or counterfeit medication, contact your local Poison Control center or emergency services immediately. Do not rely on this platform for time-sensitive medical decisions.
